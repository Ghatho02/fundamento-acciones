    Fórmulas clave

    Capital circulante neto = Current Assets − Current Liabilities

    Deuda neta = Total Debt − Cash

    NOPAT = EBIT × (1 − Tax Rate)

    Capital invertido aprox. = Debt + Equity − Cash

    ROIC aprox. = NOPAT ÷ Capital invertido

    ROIC − WACC = ROIC aprox. − WACC
